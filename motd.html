Rules

  1. Follow the Steam/Facepunch TOS
  2. Do not spam props or entities. Limit killing with props and avoid propblock.
  3. Stay respectful of other players. Settle your disputes reasonably, and take issues to staff if necessary.
  4. NSFW content of any form is not tolerated. If you have to question it, don't show it.
  5. Cheating and exploit abuse will result in a permanent ban.
  6. Do not attempt to circumvent punishment, such as disconnecting or using alternate accounts.
  7. An accessible keypad with a hold length of at least 2 seconds is required for bases.
  8. Do not abuse Wiremod, Starfall or any other addon to disrupt other players or the server. This includes attempting to gain PvP advantages.
  9. Do not lag or crash the server.
